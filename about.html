At Amity Capital Ventures, we provide support to entrepreneurs who aim to build scalable companies.

Our philosophy to make investment lies in our definition of an entrepreneur. To us, the core team should have deep interest and experience in its scope of business. We believe in backing entrepreneurs at an early stage and assist them through our association with Amity Innovation Incubator.

Our philosophy is to be a sector-agnostic capital venture. To us, the sectors should show scalable capabilities and abundant growth opportunities. We have invested across various sectors such as Education Technologies, IT Services, Clinical Research, Online Gaming, Start Automation Devices, Geographical Information Systems, Microfinance Automation, Mobile Technologies, etc.

Our philosophy is to support global entrepreneurs. To us, Entrepreneurs and Strong Management Teams with scalable ideas can be present anywhere across the globe. As a global investor, we have invested in a majority of US based companies.

Our philosophy is to provide early strategic support and be a take-off platform for companies. To us, seed/Pre-Series A investments and strategic support should enable the company to raise further rounds. We are an active strategic investor and we our closely associated with Amity Innovation Incubator to support our early stage investments.
